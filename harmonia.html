About Harmonia

First of its kind, Harmonia is an innovative amalgamation of traditional stabilizer and smart AC-controller, decked up in an affordable package. It is a home automation product which gives you remote access to all the features of your air conditioner and an ability to the power consumption as well, through a user friendly smartphone application.

Features

Harmonia is compatible with a wide range of air conditioners from different manufacturers, once you get it installed, you 'll have live control of your AC with the zo kontrol app. Along with standard voltage stabilization, Harmonia provides you a bunch of other interactive features.

  • Wifi Enabled
    Connects to Your WiFi network and Your Air Conditioner can go on to internet.
  • Smart App Control
    Harmonia can control all the features of your Air Conditioner through WiFi by Zo kontrol App.
  • Energy Efficient
    With a set of high quality components and integrated microprocessors, the product is bound to provide prolonged life and higher energy efficiency.
  • Delay Protection
    A six second delay protection window to save your Air Conditioner from initial voltage discrepancies.
  • Overload Protection
    When the load power reaches 120% of the capacity,it will automatically protect by cutting off the output.
  • Low Voltage Protection
    A below 185V output voltage initializes a safety mechanism and output supply to the appliance will be cut-off.
  • High Voltage Protection
    An above 250V output voltage will trigger a safety mechanism that automatically cuts-off the output supply to the appliance.
  • Toroidal Transformer
    It has high electrical efficiency,low Electromagnetic Interference and minimal core losses.

One App Complete Control

The Zo Kontrol app enables you to control your Air conditions from anywhere, monitor and receive notifications about temperature, humidity, schedule, energy consumption, voltage and more.
