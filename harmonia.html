About Harmonia

First of its kind, Harmonia is an innovative amalgamation of traditional stabilizer and smart AC-controller, decked up in an affordable package. It is a home automation product which gives you remote access to all the features of your air conditioner and an ability to monitor power consumption as well, through a user friendly smartphone application.

Features

Harmonia is compatible with a wide range of air conditioners from different manufacturers. Once installed, you'll have live control of your AC with the Zo kontrol app. Along with standard voltage stabilization, Harmonia provides a bunch of interactive features.

  • WiFi Enabled: Connects to your WiFi network and your air conditioner can go on to internet.
  • Smart App Control: Harmonia can control all the features of your air conditioner through WiFi by Zo kontrol app.
  • Energy Efficient: High quality components and integrated microprocessors provide prolonged life and better energy efficiency.
  • Delay Protection: Six second delay protection window saves your air conditioner from initial voltage discrepancies.
  • Overload Protection: Load power reaching 120% of capacity automatically cuts off output.
  • Low Voltage Protection: Below 185V output initializes safety mechanism and cuts output supply.
  • High Voltage Protection: Above 250V output triggers safety mechanism to cut output supply.
  • Toroidal Transformer: High efficiency, low electromagnetic interference, and minimal core losses.
